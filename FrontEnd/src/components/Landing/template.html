SIGH FINANCE : Engineered To Be Profitable!

As part of our R&D initiative, we are building SIGH FINANCE, a protocol that supports an integrated financially engineered liquidity mining pipeline controlled by A.I models to fulfill a certain objective, agreed upon by the network participants.

To fulfill its objective, Sigh Finance's liquidity mining pipeline will regulate the distibution of SIGH Tokens, an inflationary crypto-asset supporting an inflation rate which is designed to act as self-distributing store of value, across the qualifying market participants.

-> During its initial Launch, Sigh Finance's liquidity mining pipeline will cater to a pre-defined objective which involves minimizing losses (if any) incurred by the network participants due to the inherent volatility of the underlying crypto-assets.

Governance over the A.I
